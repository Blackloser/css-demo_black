公司资质 Our Qualifications     

外卖餐饮服务许可证，是上海市食品药品监督管理局为整顿外烩服务市场、升级食品安全体系、提升上海国际中心城市餐饮服务水平而推出的一项认证。从冷链工厂硬件条件到软件条件都作出了极为严格的界定，聚聚派对外烩以国内外烩行业最高规格的软硬件条件而拔得头筹，拿下了国内第一张外卖服务资格许可证，成为上海乃至中国外烩服务品味升级的标志性事件。

Take-out catering service license is a certificate system released by Shanghai Food and Drug Administration(Shanghai FDA) to regulate catering service market, update food safety system and promote Shanghai’s catering service level as an international center. In this certification system, both hardware and software of cold-chain factory are strictly required. JujuCatering has obtained the first take-out catering service license in China with its most advanced software and hardware in both domestic and international catering industry, which has become the hallmark of catering service updating in Shanghai and even in China.
